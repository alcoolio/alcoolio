Richard Kaufmann is an author and freelance journalist covering mobility, labour, and feminism. He co-founded the print magazine transform in Berlin and served on its editorial board until 2019. He subsequently published the paperback Landreisen (RAZ EL HANOUT). Further publications by Richard can be found in agora42, GEO Saison, der Freitag, and ze.tt (ZEIT Online). He holds a BA in International Communication Management from the University of Applied Sciences in Holland in Amsterdam. He lives and works in Leipzig.
