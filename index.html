Richard Kaufmann is a marketing automation consultant, helping companies and organisations design leaner and more effective marketing workflows. He can be reached via vidual.org.

Außerdem entwickelt er mit KI-Unterstützung Open-Source-Projekte mit gesellschaftlichem Anspruch. Die Arbeit ist auf GitHub einsehbar.

He also uses AI-assisted programming to build open-source projects with a social purpose. The work is available on GitHub.

Weitere Veröffentlichungen von ihm sind bei agora42, GEO Saison, der Freitag und ze.tt (ZEIT Online) erschienen. Er hat einen BA in International Communication Management von der University of Applied Sciences in Holland in Amsterdam und lebt in Leipzig.

Before that, he worked as an author and freelance journalist, writing about mobility, labour, and feminism. He co-founded the print magazine transform in Berlin and served on its editorial board until 2019, then published the paperback Landreisen (RAZ EL HANOUT). Further texts have appeared in agora42, GEO Saison, der Freitag, and ze.tt (ZEIT Online). He holds a BA in International Communication Management from the University of Applied Sciences in Holland in Amsterdam and lives in Leipzig.
